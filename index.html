Authentic Telugu Curries and Pappu/Dals for Every Palate!

From Dondakaya Fry to Pappu Charu, We Have It All!!!

Indulge in the rich and flavorful curries and pappu also known as lentils of Telangana cuisine and my family's with our selection of authentic dishes. From the crispy and spicy dondakaya fry and bendakaya fry to the wholesome and comforting palakura pappu, tomato pappu, and pesar pappu, we have something for every taste. Don't miss out on the tangy and delicious pappu charu or the classic aloo fry, perfect for any meal. Made with the freshest ingredients and traditional techniques, our Telugu curries and dals are sure to delight your senses.

Satisfy Your Sweet Tooth with Authentic Telugu Sweets

From Poornalu to Rasagulla, We Have a Sweet for Every Occasion!!!

Satisfy your sweet tooth with the rich and aromatic flavors of our authentic Telugu dessert, Carrot Halwa. Made with grated carrots, milk, ghee, and a blend of traditional spices, this sweet treat is a comforting and indulgent delight. Looking for something with a bit more crunch? Try our traditional Poornalu, made with jaggery and coconut stuffing and coated with a crispy outer layer. Sample my family's collection of authentic Telugu sweets and discover the rich and varied flavors of our cuisine.
