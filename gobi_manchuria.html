Gobi Manchuria

Gobi Manchurian is a popular Indo-Chinese dish made with cauliflower florets, coated in a batter and deep-fried until crispy, and then stir-fried with a sauce made from soy sauce, chili sauce, garlic, and other spices. The dish is commonly served as an appetizer or as a side dish with fried rice or noodles. Gobi Manchurian is a flavorful and spicy dish that has become very popular in India and other parts of the world. It is rich in nutrients such as fiber, vitamin C, and vitamin K, as well as minerals such as potassium and magnesium.

And this is how my mum makes Gobi Manchuria...

For one person the measurements and procedure for Telugu Chicken Biryani

1 head of cauliflower, cut into small florets

1/2 cup all-purpose flour

1/4 cup cornstarch

1 tsp ginger paste

1 tsp garlic paste

1 tbsp soy sauce

1 tbsp hot chili sauce

1 tsp cumin powder

1 tsp coriander powder

1 tsp garam masala

Salt, to taste

Oil, for deep frying

2 tbsp oil

1 onion, chopped

1 capsicum/bell pepper, chopped

2 green chilies, chopped

1 tbsp ginger paste

1 tbsp garlic paste

2 tbsp soy sauce

2 tbsp hot chili sauce

1 tsp vinegar

1 tsp sugar

1/2 cup water

1 tbsp cornstarch slurry (1 tbsp cornstarch mixed with 2 tbsp water)

2 tbsp chopped spring onions for garnish

PROCEDURE

STEP 1:

In a mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour, cornstarch, ginger paste, garlic paste, soy sauce, hot chili sauce, cumin powder, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and add water as required to make a thick batter.

STEP 2:

Add the cauliflower florets to the batter and mix until all the florets are coated with the batter.

STEP 3:

Heat oil in a deep frying pan over medium-high heat. Once the oil is hot, carefully add the cauliflower florets to the oil and fry until golden brown and crispy. Drain the fried cauliflower on paper towels to remove excess oil.

STEP 4:

In a separate pan, heat 2 tablespoons of oil over medium-high heat. Add the chopped onions, capsicum/bell pepper, and green chilies. Sauté for a few minutes until the vegetables are slightly tender.

STEP 5:

Add the ginger paste and garlic paste and sauté for a minute.

STEP 6:

Add soy sauce, hot chili sauce, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Mix well and cook for a minute.

STEP 7:

Add water and bring the mixture to a boil. Reduce the heat and simmer for a few minutes.

STEP 8:

Add the cornstarch slurry to the mixture and stir continuously until the sauce thickens.

STEP 9:

Add the fried cauliflower florets to the sauce and toss until all the florets are coated with the sauce.

STEP 10:

Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
