CAPSICUM MASALA

Capsicum masala is a popular vegetarian Indian dish made with capsicum (bell peppers) and a tomato-based gravy. It is typically served with chapati or rice. The dish is easy to prepare and is a great way to incorporate bell peppers into your diet. Capsicum is a good source of vitamins A and C, and is also low in calories, making it a healthy addition to any meal. Overall, capsicum masala is a flavorful and nutritious dish that is enjoyed by many in India and beyond.

The following is how my family makes capsicum masala...

BASIC INGREDIENTS:

Capsicum (bell peppers)

Onion

Tomato

Green chillies

Ginger-garlic paste

Turmeric powder

Red chilli powder

Coriander powder

Cumin seeds

Mustard seeds

Curry leaves

Salt

Oil

Sesame seeds (nuvvulu)

Groundnut (pallilu) paste

For one person the measurements and procedure for Telugu Chicken Biryani

Capsicum/Bell pepper - 2 large, chopped into small pieces

Onion - 1 large, finely chopped

Tomatoes - 2 medium, finely chopped

Green chillies - 2, finely chopped

Ginger garlic paste - 1 tsp

Red chilli powder - 1 tsp

Turmeric powder - 1/4 tsp

Coriander powder - 1 tsp

Cumin powder - 1/2 tsp

Garam masala powder - 1/2 tsp

Salt - to taste

Oil - 2 tbsp

Mustard seeds - 1/2 tsp

Cumin seeds - 1/2 tsp

Curry leaves - 1 sprig

Sesame seeds - 1 tbsp, roasted and ground to a paste

Groundnut paste - 2 tbsp

PROCEDURE

STEP 1:

Heat oil in a pan and add mustard seeds. Once the seeds start to splutter, add cumin seeds and curry leaves.

STEP 2:

Add the chopped onions and sauté until they turn translucent.

STEP 3:

Add the ginger garlic paste and green chillies, and sauté for a minute.

STEP 4:

Add the chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.

STEP 5:

Add the chopped capsicum and mix well. Cook for 5-6 minutes until they are slightly soft.

STEP 6:

Add red chilli powder, turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, garam masala powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.

STEP 7:

Add the sesame seed paste and groundnut paste to the pan and mix well.

STEP 8:

Add some water to the pan to adjust the consistency of the masala. Cook for 5-7 minutes until the masala is well blended and the capsicum is completely cooked.

STEP 9:

Serve hot with roti or rice.
