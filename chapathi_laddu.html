Chapathi Laddu

Chapathi Laddu is a sweet dish that is commonly made during festivals in India. It is a popular dessert in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana. Chapathi Laddu is a delicious and nutritious dessert that is rich in carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats. It is also a good source of dietary fiber and essential vitamins and minerals.

And this is how my mum makes Chapathi Laddu...

BASIC INGREDIENTS:

Chapatis (leftover or fresh)

White sugar

Ghee (clarified butter)

Cardamom powder

Cashew nuts (chopped)

Raisins

Grated coconut

For one person the measurements and procedure for Telugu Chicken Biryani

5-6 chapathis

1 or 2 cups white sugar

1/2 cup grated coconut

1/4 cup roasted and chopped cashews

1/4 cup raisins

1/4 tsp cardamom powder

3 tbsp ghee

PROCEDURE

STEP 1:

Make chapathi's and tear them into small pieces. You can also use a grinder to make them smaller.

STEP 2:

In a bowl, add the torn chapathi pieces, ghee, white sugar, grated coconut, roasted and chopped cashews, and raisins (optional).

STEP 3:

Add cardamom powder to the mixture and mix everything well using your hands.

STEP 4:

Take a small portion of the mixture and shape it into a laddu using your palms.

STEP 5:

Repeat the process with the remaining mixture to make more laddus.

STEP 6:

Store the laddus in an airtight container and serve as a sweet snack.
