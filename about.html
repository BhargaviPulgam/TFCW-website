ABOUT ME

"Welcome to My Telugu Fam's Cooking Wonders! Our website is dedicated to sharing delicious Telugu recipes that have been passed down through generations in our family. We believe that food is not only nourishment for the body but also a way to bring people together and celebrate our culture and heritage."

I'm Bhargavi Pulgam from Hyderabad, India. My aunt and grandparents introduced me to Telugu cooking. I cherish the recipes and techniques they taught me, even after moving to Leicester, England, to study. This website shares traditional recipes to inspire you to explore Telugu cuisine's diverse flavors.

WHAT CAN YOU FIND IN THIS WEBSITE?

On our website, you can find a variety of traditional Telugu food recipes along with authentic ones. The recipes are designed with easy-to-follow steps, making it suitable for beginners to cook with ease. As a dynamic platform, we upload new recipes biweekly or tri-weekly, providing a range of exciting dishes to explore. Come and discover the delicious world of Telugu Indian cooking with us.

MY INSPIRATION

The inspiration behind this website is deeply rooted in my family's heritage and my passion for cooking. My great-grandmother, affectionately known as "avva," was a master in the kitchen and prepared a wide variety of traditional Telugu dishes whenever we visited her in our village of Alair. Her knowledge of food and cooking techniques was vast and she instilled in me a deep appreciation for Telugu cuisine. She encouraged me to learn and experiment with different dishes, and her inspiration led me to create this platform to share her recipes and bring the flavors of Telugu cooking to a wider audience.
